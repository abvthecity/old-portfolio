The Helvetica Magazine

Leland High School (Club)
May 2014 — June 2015
Andrew Jiang (Design Editor)

While ruminating about our next project after completing the magazine, Tanveer Bains and I decided to produce Leland High School's first magazine publication. Our idea was inspired by Brandon Stanton's Humans of New York, but we wanted to delve deeper and give students and teachers of Leland High School a chance to read student biographies and learn more about the "hidden side" of their peers. We chose the name Helvetica because it symbolized hidden and unappreciated beauty, analogous to the people at our school. To match the simplicity of the typeface we wanted to represent the magazine, I designed a grid for a minimalistic layout that used Helvetica for headings and (ironically) Charter for copy. Below, I've included two original grids I made for the design, and the final design exported from InDesign. I also included a link to the PDF of each issue.
