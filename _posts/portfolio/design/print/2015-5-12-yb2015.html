Lost In The World

If Found Please Return

LEGEND / Yearbook 2015
Leland High School
August 2014 — May 2015

Editor in Chief — Barbara Yang
Design Editor — Andrew Jiang
Photo Editor — Tanveer Bains

Heading into seniordom, college apps became more and more important to us editors. We bonded over this chaotic period of our lives, but the yearbook still needed to get done. A few things I learned from yearbook this year is that patience is not always a virtue. If it leads to procrastination or lateness, take more authority. Have a little fun, and poke fun at yourself. Do something crazy to make the experience more memorable, even if it means traveling 60 miles to take a few photos in San Francisco (one of my many crazy ideas). We had a lot of fun making this yearbook, piling it with jokes about Kanye West. Indeed, the title was inspired by Kanye, but it also represents what all of us felt this year.
